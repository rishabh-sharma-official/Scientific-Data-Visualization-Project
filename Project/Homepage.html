Conclusion

After analyzing the visualizations and dashboards presented above, it's evident that population density plays a significant role in the spread of COVID-19. Countries with higher population densities tend to experience more rapid transmission rates. Additionally, access to medical resources, such as doctors per 1000 people, varies across different regions, impacting the ability to control the spread effectively.
